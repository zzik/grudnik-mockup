ABOUT

Photo by Someone

Hi, I'm an illustrator and graphic designer from Ljubljana, Slovenia.

CLIENTS include: Mestno gledališče Ljubljansko (Ljubljana City Theatre), Columbia Univeristy of the Arts - Theatre Department (NY), Anton Podbevšek Teater, Union Hotels (Hotel Lev), Air Beletrina, Cankarjev dom, Mestni muzej Ljubljana (City Museum of Ljubljana), Javna agencija za knjgo RS (Slovenian Book Agency), Mladinska knjiga, Outsider magazine, Cacao Europa magazine, Likovne besede (ArtWords), A1 Slovenija, Flaviar, Next In Line Productions NYC, Invisible Wall Productions NYC, Medprostor - Arhitekturni atelje, Zavod Exodos Ljubljana, Slovenska kinoteka, Tribuna, Tuš, Spirit Slovenija (Public Agency for Entrepreneurship, Internationalization, Fore­ign Investments and Technology), Narodna galerija (National Gallery of Slovenia), VigeVageKnjige, Beletrina, Založba Didakta, Wellcome Collection (London), Fine Acts

I studied History of Art (Faculty of Arts, Ljubljana) and also Visual Communication at the Academy of Fine Arts and Design in Ljubljana. In my work, I love to combine handmade and digital processes for a fresh, artful approach and enjoy playing with different techniques. Inspired by books, museums, medieval times and tiny little details. I live and work in Ljubljana, Slovenia.
